If someone leaves:
	- skip on their turn
	- cannot be inquired

If no response in time limit, turn is skipped

When someone claims an action:
	- Server notifies all players of action claimed
	- Players have 10s to respond, default is no
	- Players are not notified of others' response
	- Outcome is revealed at the end

When someone is executing an action:
	- If not input is received, they lose their action

When someone chooses to swap or not:
	- Broadcast target
